Too good? Maybe not true!

Keep an open mind!
If some sort of information seems way too out of the blue, it may be fake. Seek out suspicious statements through details like numbers, people, places, and more.

No author? No credibility!

Look for any author/editor!
Look through the site and seek any authors or methods of communication (i.e. email) with those who wrote the article. Investigate further if needed.

Lateral Reading!

Don't stick to just one page!
Check out related articles to see if the information on stays consistent. If there aren't any other articles, you should be very suspicious. (This exercise will require you to read laterally!)
